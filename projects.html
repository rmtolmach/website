Kale Salad

  • one bunch of kale
  • juice of one lemon
  • parmesan cheese
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper
  1. Wash kale to your standards. I take the extra time to inspect each leaf to make sure I don't end up eating any aphids. But that's a personal preference.
  2. With or without aphids, chop kale into bite-sized pieces. Massage your kale if you'd like.
  3. whisk lemon juice, salt and pepper together and then drizzle in olive oil.
  4. Pour dressing and shredded parmesan over kale, mix and devour.
